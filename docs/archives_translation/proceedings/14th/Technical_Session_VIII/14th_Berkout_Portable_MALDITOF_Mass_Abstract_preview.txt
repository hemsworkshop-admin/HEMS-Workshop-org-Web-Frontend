Portable MALDI-TOF Mass Spectrometer for Bioaerosol Detection Vadym Berkout, Stuart Collymore, Scott Ecelberger, Mike McLoughlin, Wayne Bryden, et al. Zeteo Tech, Inc., 6935 Warfield Ave, Sykesville, MD 21784 Rapid, real-time pathogen identification is required to protect humans from either natural or manmade aerosolized biological particles, including bacteria, viruses and toxins. In a biothreat detection system based on MALDI (matrix assisted laser desorption/ionization) mass spectrometry, developed at Zeteo, aerosol is deposited by virtual impact concentrator on a reusable metal disk.

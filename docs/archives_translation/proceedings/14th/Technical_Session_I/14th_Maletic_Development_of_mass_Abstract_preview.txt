Development of mass spectrometer for organic molecule detection in the future space life search missions Dejan Maletic, Victor Abrahamsson, Jurij Simcic, Dragan Nikolic, Stojan Madzunkov NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of Technology, 4800 Oak Grove Dr, Pasadena, CA 91109 The search for extraterrestrial life on planetary bodies, such as “Ocean worlds” and Mars, is a very challenging task. The ideal instrument for this task must be small in weight and size, but also very sensitive to detect very low concentrations of organic molecules.

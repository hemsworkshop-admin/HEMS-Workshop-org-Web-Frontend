Breath Markers of Disease Identifiers for Portable Mass Spectrometry Guido Verbeck University of North Texas Breath capture chemistry is a rising method of examining physiological information as a means to discover and track biomarkers and metabolites for clinical applications. Previous studies have highlighted the ability of breath samples to indicate presence of respiratory viral infections, such as Influenza A, MERS, SARS-CoV-1, and in our group SARS-CoV-2, through the detection of volatile chemical markers via mass spectrometry (MS).

An optimized membrane inlet system (MIS) for underwater sensors - From idea to product Torben Gentz, Malte Höhn Alfred-Wegener-Institute for Polar and Marine Research, Bremerhaven, Germany The pressure resistance in the deep ocean is most important for in-situ measurements. In case of the underwater mass-spectrometry (UWMS) especially the requirements of the combination of high permeability for fast low detection limits and stable structures for pressure resistance in the membrane- inlet-systems (MIS) are challenging.

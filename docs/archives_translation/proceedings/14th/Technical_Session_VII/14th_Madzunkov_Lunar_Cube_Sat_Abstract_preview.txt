Lunar Cube Sat Mass Spectrometer Stojan Madzunkov, Dragan Nikolic Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Caltech Lunar Cube Sat Mass Spectrometer (LCMS) is a Paul trap mass spectrometer, based on JPL's Quadrupole Ion Trap Mass Spectrometer (QITMS), develop and build (DALI) to operate on the Lunar surface. Its main function is to measure the content of the Lunar exosphere (in the mass range 2 -150amu with resolution of 1000) in the open ionizer architecture (no pumping).
